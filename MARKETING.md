## What is Walory?

**Walory** is a modern web application built for collectors of all kinds – coins, stamps, jewellery, antiques, and more. Whether you are just starting your collection or have been collecting for decades, Walory gives you the tools to manage, explore, and share your passion.

---

## ✨ Key Features

### 📦 Manage Your Collection
- Add, edit, and remove collectibles with ease.
- Organize items into custom **categories** (coins, stamps, banknotes, jewellery, etc.).
- Define your own **data models** per category – track fields that matter to *you*, such as year of issue, condition, mint, or rarity.

### 🔍 Find What You Need
- **Filter and sort** your collectibles by any attribute.
- Quickly locate any item in even the largest collection.

### 📤 Export Your Data
- Export your collection data to **PDF** or **XLSX** – perfect for insurance, appraisal, or trading.

### 🤝 Connect with Fellow Collectors
- Invite friends by email and share your collection with them.
- Browse friends' collections and leave **comments** on individual items.

### 🔒 Secure & Reliable
- Account creation and login secured with **OAuth2**.
- All data stored in a robust **relational database (PostgreSQL)**.
